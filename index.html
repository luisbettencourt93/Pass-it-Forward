Members list their own fundraises with their own Stripe link. Money goes directly to them — Pass It Forward never touches it. We just provide the visibility.

Have a project that needs funding?

List your raise here. You provide your own Stripe Payment Link — members can back you directly. The money goes straight to your Stripe account, not ours.

How Member Raises work: You create a Stripe Payment Link from your own Stripe account. Paste it here when listing your raise. When someone clicks "Back This" they go to your Stripe — you get the money, Pass It Forward is not involved in the transaction.

Specific initiatives run by Pass It Forward. Each project has its own fund — members can see exactly what money is raised for and how it's being spent.

Founder-controlled: Project funds are created and managed by the Pass It Forward founder. All Stripe payments for these projects go to the community Stripe account. Members see full transparency on goals and spending — but only the founder can create or allocate project funds.
